Shower at 548 El Camino

This page provides information relevant to fixing the downstairs shower at 548 El Camino, Sedona, AZ US.

The problem with the shower (and tub) is that the temperature of the water cannot be changed. A technician from Goettl's came out and said that the problem was that some parts are missing inside the Grohe shower control.

I have made PDFs from the two Grohe manuals from when the new shower controls were installed on 16 September 2024. The manuals are available here:
